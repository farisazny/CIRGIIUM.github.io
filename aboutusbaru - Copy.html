About CIRG IIUM

The research group is currently engaged in a study of the challenges that are encountered in artificial intelligence as a result of the computational analysis of comprehensive data. Their primary emphasis is on developing solutions through computational modelling approaches, machine learning, and deep learning algorithms.


The study focuses on machine learning, deep learning, optimisation, Natural Language Processing (NLP), data science and other artificial intelligence techniques and methods. The research work focuses on machine learning, deep learning, optimisation, Natural Language Processing (NLP), data science and other artificial intelligence techniques and methods.


it comprise :

    * Medical Imaging: In the healthcare industry, machine learning techniques facilitate the examination and interpretation of complex medical images, resulting in expedited and more accurate diagnostic outcomes. The utilisation of this technology enables the identification of early indicators of disease, hence permitting prompt interventions.

    * Data privacy: Data privacy preservation has become increasingly crucial in light of the exponential growth of digital data. Machine learning techniques have emerged as a reliable means of ensuring solid safeguards for data security. Methods such as differential privacy are employed to obfuscate individual-level data, preserving the information's usefulness while effectively safeguarding against potential breaches and unauthorised access.

    * Optimisation: Machine learning algorithms are crucial in optimising intricate systems, ranging from supply chains to network topologies, by forecasting optimal outcomes and enhancing operational efficiency.


Machine learning in data analysis facilitates the exploration and identification of concealed patterns and insights, enhancing the field of data science and visualisation. Moreover, it enables the visualisation of extensive datasets in easily understandable formats, improving the accessibility of data-driven decision-making.
